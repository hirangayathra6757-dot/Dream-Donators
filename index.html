Help a Sri Lankan child walk into school with confidence

We collect and distribute school uniforms, books, shoes and bags to students affected by the pandemic. Join our community of Dream Donators — every item matters.

Become a Donator Request Help (Students)

How it works

Donators register their item donations. Students in need submit a help request. Administrators match items to requests and coordinate delivery.

Who we help

Primary & secondary students across schools in Sri Lanka, prioritising the most vulnerable communities affected by recent crises.

Transparency

We publish anonymized monthly reports and photos. Admins can export donor and request lists for accountability.

About Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students across Sri Lanka by providing essential school supplies and support. We ensure each child has the opportunity to learn and thrive — donations are tracked and distributed by our team of volunteers.
